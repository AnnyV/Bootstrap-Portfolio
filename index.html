About Me

My name is Ana Visconti and I am a Web Developer. My interest for coding started at the age of 15 years old when I studied Computer Programming at CENTU Computer Institute in the Domincan Republic. I've been coding for the last 4 months and created a total of 7 applications ranging from front end, purely back end to full stack and mobile friendly.

My favorite application so far is The hangtoons Hangman game. It is a Hangman game that reminds you of Saturday mornings with your favorite cartoons. I love to see the faces of people reliving those moments.

The second one is a Civic Trivia Game, inspired by my mother who was studying for her citizenship test and always needed somebody or something to help her practice. I shared my trivia app with a couple of friends on Facebook. I believe it can help many people.

I also very blessed because I can count on the support of a great mentor, My husband Vincent Visconti. He has been a developer for over 18 years. He is helping me to materialize my dreams and guiding me on this amazing journey.
